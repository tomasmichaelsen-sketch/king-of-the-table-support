Quick FAQ

MatchesMatch not updating?

  • Make sure both players have confirmed the result.
  • Pull down to refresh the app or reopen it.

TestFlightNot seeing the latest build?

  • Open TestFlight and pull down to refresh.
  • Enable “Automatic Updates” in TestFlight.
  • If you’re an external tester, the new build may be awaiting Beta Review.

AccountsCan’t sign in?

  • Check network connection and try again.
  • If the issue persists, email support with a screenshot.
